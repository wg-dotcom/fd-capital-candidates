What FD Capital Needs. Who Can Actually Deliver It.

An M&A advisory firm at FD Capital's level doesn't just need someone who can process invoices. They need someone who handles financial data with the same level of discretion applied to every deal on the table. AR/AP in this environment means managing payments tied to active transactions, confidential vendor relationships, and client billing that can't have errors.

I focused on candidates who have worked in environments where financial accuracy wasn't a nice-to-have — it was the baseline. People who've managed multi-entity accounts, dealt with US-facing billing cycles, and know their way around QuickBooks, Xero, or equivalent tools. Attention to detail, professional English, and ownership over the numbers without needing to be managed. That's the filter.

Eight candidates in this batch. Philippines-heavy (strong AR/AP pipeline there), plus talent from West Africa, East Africa, and South Asia. All remote-ready, all with solid AR/AP foundations. Intro videos are pending — I'm collecting them now and will update this page as they come in. Every profile here tells the story on paper.
