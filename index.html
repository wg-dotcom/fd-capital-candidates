What FD Capital Needs. Who Can Actually Deliver It.

FD Capital runs 81 stores across Wingstop and Jersey Mike's, with 20 more in the pipeline this year. At ~$130M in annual sales and 96 locations by year-end, the AP function touches every store, every vendor, every POS reconciliation. This is not a back-office afterthought. It's the financial engine that keeps a fast-scaling franchise platform running cleanly.

You need someone who can sit under the US-based controller and own the AP workload full-time. Invoice processing, vendor payments, bank reconciliations, cash management across dozens of entities. The right person slots in, runs their lane, and does not need to be managed. Accuracy, speed, and the discipline to handle high-volume transaction work 40 hours a week without dropping anything. That's the filter.

Four candidates in this batch. All based in the Philippines, all remote-ready, all with hands-on AP experience in real companies. Every profile here has an intro video and tells the story on paper.
